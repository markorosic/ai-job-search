## Interview Prep Notes
- Address the agency context directly and proactively: "I'm moving from in-house product to an environment where I can apply design systems thinking across diverse ecosystems. AKQA's tech client roster is where I see the tightest overlap with what I've built."
- Lead with multi-brand, multi-market scale: Catena 10+ websites + Gentoo multi-brand system — closest analogue to serving multiple clients

- Ask about the ratio of new business vs. retained client work — this affects the IC stability of the Director role
