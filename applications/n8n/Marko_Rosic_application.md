## Verdict: Strong Fit — good call applying

Clearly stronger than GotPhoto (72, culture red flag): sector bullseye, explicit DE visa sponsorship, Head-level seat, healthy culture. The real risk is **execution/credibility on the developer-tools domain** plus a **scope step-up**, not alignment.

### Key strengths
- Head-of-design with **managed-managers** experience — directly parallels n8n's "2 design leads each running ~6 ICs."
- **AI-orchestration edge (Claude Code, Figma plugins, tooling)** maps onto "champion practical AI adoption" and "AI-era product judgment" — a differentiator most design leaders won't have.
- Design systems / craft standards / continuous research / data-informed decisions — all core.
- Logistically ideal: Germany (primary) + visa sponsorship + remote-first + English.

### Gaps to address (interview prep, since already applied)
1. **Developer-facing product domain** (the #1 gap) — he hasn't designed *for developers as users*. Counter with design-engineering fluency, plugin building, technical literacy, and AI-tooling work to show he operates at the dev boundary even though his end-users were consumers.
2. **Scope step-up** (~14 + design eng + research vs his 7 + 2 leads) — frame Gentoo as the same shape at smaller scale; show readiness to scale org design + performance management.
3. **Hyper-growth pace** ($5.2B, rapid headcount) — probe expectations/stability; lean on his systematic, "ruthless prioritization" temperament.
4. Consumer/affiliate → dev-tools narrative; never centre gambling.
